the description of spaghetti

it is thin, solid, cylindrical pasta.It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine.


The history of spaghetti

In the United States around the end of the 19th century, spaghetti was offered in restaurants as Spaghetti Italienne and it was not until decades later that it came to be commonly prepared with oregano or basil.

types of spaghetti

  • spaghettini (thinner)
  • spaghettoni (thicker)
  • bucatini (thicker and straw-like, with a hollow center)
  • capellini (very thin)
  • angel's hair (thinnest).
